Tesla's Autopilot technology has seen significant advancements in recent years. One of the latest features in Tesla vehicles is the company's Full Self-Driving (FSD) technology. This technology, which is still in development, aims to provide a higher level of autonomy for Tesla vehicles. As Tesla's Autopilot, Enhanced Autopilot, and Full Self-Driving capabilities evolve, cars are continuously upgraded through over-the-air software updates.

In 2023, Tesla has made several improvements to its Autopilot technology. The company has started to release a new Full Self-Driving Beta update with some performance improvements. Although it doesn't look like a huge leap forward, it demonstrates Tesla's commitment to refining and enhancing its autonomous driving capabilities.

Tesla continues to innovate in the field of electric vehicles, with a focus on battery range and autonomous driving. As the technology progresses, Tesla aims to make driving safer, more efficient, and more enjoyable for its customers.
